MINISTRIES

Abba Modern
Abba Modern is UC Berkeley's only Christ-centered dance team with a vision to spread God's love through the art of dance and testimony.

Alpha Delta Chi (ADX)
ADX is UC Berkeley's only Christian sorority focusing on our growth as sisters spiritually, scholastically, and socially.

Alpha Gamma Omega (AGO)
We are a Christ-Centered social fraternity on college campuses. Our brotherhood seeks to know, love, and glorify God and be used by Him to establish, equip, and encourage lifelong followers of God.

Asian American Christian Fellowship (AACF)
The mission of Asian American Christian Fellowship is to reach into the university and collegiate community, primarily to those who are Asian Pacific Americans, with the life changing message of Jesus Christ.

Crossroads
Crossroads is a ministry of Vinewood CFC Berkeley that is a student-led Christian fellowship on the UC Berkeley campus.

Cru
Berkeley Cru is a multicultural student community that follows Jesus Christ and takes the gospel to the world.

Fellowship in Christ Berkeley
Fellowship in Christ Berkeley (FiCB) is the campus ministry of Christ Central Presbyterian Church. We are a family in Christ that proclaims the gospel and supports each other in growing in Christ likeness everyday.

For Christ's Sake A Cappella
As the Christian a cappella group at UC Berkeley, we have come together across campus fellowships and churches to proclaim the good news of the Gospel through song to our campus and community.

Global Medical Missions Alliance (GMMA)
Welcome to Global Medical Missions Alliance @ Berkeley! We are committed to:

  • Create a unique, close-knit community for pre-health students
  • Offer medical missions and community outreach opportunities for pre-health students
  • Provide guidance from Christian mentors for students aspiring to become healthcare professionals dedicated to expanding God’s Kingdom

    • Imago Dei
    Imago Dei Clinic is a Christian nonprofit committed to saving lives and empowering the homeless community by providing harm-reduction services and sharing Biblical truth. The name, “Imago Dei,” refers to the Greek phrase “Image of God,” recognizing the God-given worth of every individual that we serve. Through our weekly outreach events, we hope to share the love of Christ with the people we meet through consistent relational ministry and reduce the risk of opioid overdose deaths within the community.

    InterVarsity Cal Christian Fellowship (IV CCF)
    In CCF we want to follow Jesus as a community of faith, prayer, and discipleship. We try to provide an open space to engage the campus in the exploration of faith.

    Kerusso Fellowship
    Kerusso Fellowship is a UC Berkeley on-campus Christian college fellowship of NorthCreek Church in Walnut Creek. We are committed to Personal Sanctification, Bible Study, Biblical Fellowship, Service Opportunities, and Local Church involvement. We hold weekly bible studies as a time for us to learn from God's Word, worship in praise, and fellowship. NorthCreek Church has two Worship Services each Sunday at 9:00am and 10:30am. Many of us attend Worship Service at 9:00 and Innerchange (college and career singles group) at 10:30.

    Klesis
    We’re Klesis, a UC Berkeley Christian campus fellowship. Klesis is a Greek word that means “divine invitation.” College is full of invitations, but this is a special one. So whether you’re a Christian who wants to grow in your faith, or just curious about what Christianity is all about, we invite you to join us!
    We offer different life groups for anyone to enjoy! From going on weekend outings and adventures to weekly study halls and dinners, Klesis has many opportunities to meet with other students and even mentors to have fun and make life easier!

    Laundry Love
    We are a Christian nonprofit organization run by UC Berkeley students that aims to share the gospel to the homeless through providing laundry service.

    Pursuit Fellowship
    We are Pursuit Fellowship from Livingwater Church (church name gonna change soon btw). A ministry where every student is united and cultivating a missional lifestyle of proactive love. We promote and encourage growth, maturity, edification, accountability, and worship of God. “But seek first the kingdom of God and his righteousness, and all these things will be added to you.””- Matthew 6:33

    Reformed University Fellowship (RUF)
    Welcome to RUF at Berkeley! We are a student organization that strives to take the Gospel seriously (the good news of Jesus Christ), without taking ourselves too seriously. We believe it is the Gospel that gives our lives meaning, and it is the Gospel that brings about change, healing, and renewal in our world.
    There's a place for everyone at RUF! Whether you are a cynic, a skeptic, a new believer, a life-long Christian, or just someone who is weary with all of life's challenges and looking for hope and encouragement, we would love to meet you and get to know you!

    Soon Movement Global
    SOON Movement Global is an interdenominational non-profit missionary order committed to spreading the life-changing Gospel of Jesus Christ to every campus, community, and nation as a member of the global movement of Campus Crusade for Christ International. Our calling is to help fulfill the Great Commission. Our vision is to build spiritual movement everywhere with the Gospel of Jesus Christ. Our mission is to win, build, and send Spirit-filled disciples everywhere through multiethnic movements. Our core values are faith, growth, and fruitfulness.

    Spero
    We are a UC Berkeley student-run philanthropic fashion company focused on sharing the Gospel message!

    To An Unknown God
    To An Unknown God is UC Berkeley’s first student-run Christian journal. Every semester, we publish writing and artwork produced by Cal students, hoping to foster dialogue between Christians and students of other faiths and philosophies.

    Veritas
    Veritas Graduate Christian Fellowship is a community of graduate students and academics from diverse theological backgrounds seeking to understand and live out the Christian faith together with love and humility at the UC Berkeley campus.

    YHWH Apparel
    YHWH Apparel is a Christ-centered nonprofit with the mission of sharing God’s name on Berkeley campus and beyond through clothing! Each product release is intentionally designed and rooted in studies of Scripture.
